Frequently asked questions

1. Do you buy books from the public?

Yes, we buy books! Cash paid for books in excellent condition. Please call to discuss.

2. What is your returns policy?

Your satisfaction is very important to us. If you are not 100% happy with your purchase, we are happy to refund the full purchase price, minus postage (if any) and insurance costs. Simply return the product in its original condition within one month of your original invoice date, and we will arrange for your reimbursement.

3. What is your Privacy policy?

Should you contact us with a question, comment or suggestion, we will only use your email address and/or telephone number to answer your enquiry or send you our newsletter. We will not disclose your personal details to any other person or company.

4. Are you a chain bookshop?

No. We are not a chain bookshop at this stage.

5. How can I apply for a job at your shop?

We advertise job vacancies in our local newsletter and sometimes in the Geelong Advertiser newspaper. We also are happy to accept applications from people at any time - whether we currently have a vacancy or not.
