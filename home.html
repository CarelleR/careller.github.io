History

In August 2001, Lauren and Shaun opened this used book shop at 123 Moorabool Street in Geelong. In 2015, we started opening at 9 am. We must have been the only book shop open at this hour! Today, we have a range of secondhand books and selected new ones.

Sell

Do you have books to sell? We're always on the look out for secondhand books. Do try us if you have any you no longer require, we may well be interested, for cash or exchange. Please phone first to arrange a convenient time.
